### Volunteers for Workshop
- For short workshop (~2 hrs)
	- If the organizers can provide volunteers, the workshop would ideally want 1 helper per 2~3 tables so that we can distribute attention to every participants with their progress.

### Workshop Fundamentals
- Flat Tables (4 person per table) with enough space for all participants to work on
- Extension Power Cables and Power Strips
- A PA system for presentations, experiments, and the final performance
- A projector and a screen for presentations
- Possibly fire extinguisher just in case something catches on fire

### Tools Needed for Workshop
- Soldering stations (As many as possible)
	- Solder
	- Soldering Iron
	- Desoldering wick
	- Magnifier (Optional)
- Cutting tools (As many as possible)
	- Wire Stripper
	- Wire Cutter
	- Scissor
	- Exato Knife
- Multimeter
- Super Glue
- Water Spray
